About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Purrfect Paws started with a simple dream: to create a space where cat lovers can relax over a cup of coffee while being surrounded by adorable feline friends.

All our cats are rescue cats from local London shelters. We work closely with partner charities to provide a forever home for each of our furry residents.
